Welcome To Lodha Riservo

Elevate your lifestyle at Lodha Riservo, a luxury residential address located in Vikhroli. The project offers fully air-conditioned 3, 3.5 and 4.5 BHK flats equipped with spacious study rooms & decks. The apartments also feature premium bath fittings & high-quality kitchen platforms. The 25,000 sq. ft clubhouse adds grandeur to the project while Jain & Ganesha temple, adds a serene vibe to the project. Based right at LBS Road in the vibrant locale of Vikhroli West, this project offers excellent access to some key landmarks in the city. These include R City Mall, Hiranandani Hospital, & Orchids The International School - all within a 5 to 10-minute drive! Quick access to LBS Marg connects you to prominent business hubs in Mumbai, while the Vikhroli Station & Eastern Express Highway are a short distance away as well. With lavish homes & ultra-modern amenities - Lodha Riservo is your entry to a luxurious lifestyle in a prime location.
